# Retail-Sales-Customer-Analytics-Dashboard-
Retail-Sales: Airbnb Customer-Analytics-Dashboard
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Airbnb data, highlighting key metrics like total profit, average pricing, and booking distribution. It helps identify high-performing locations, pricing trends, and customer preferences. The dashboard enables data-driven decisions for optimizing pricing and maximizing revenue.

Description:
This dashboard visualizes Airbnb data to analyze profit, pricing trends, and booking patterns across different locations. It highlights top-performing neighborhoods, property types, and room categories. The insights help in understanding market demand and optimizing business strategies..

Purpose:
The purpose of this dashboard is to analyze Airbnb data and uncover key insights related to pricing, bookings, and profitability. It helps stakeholders identify high-demand locations, optimize pricing strategies, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance.

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
Power BI Desktop - Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
Power Query - Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) - Used for calculation measures, dynamic visuals and conditional logic.
Data Modeling - Relationships established among tables to enable cross-filtering and aggregation.
File Format - .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

Data sources: Kaggle
The dataset consists of Airbnb listing and calendar data, including details like property type, location, price, availability, and host information. It captures booking patterns, pricing variations over time, and neighborhood-wise distribution. These datasets enable analysis of market trends, customer preferences, and revenue opportunities.

Business problem
The business faces challenges in identifying optimal pricing, high-demand locations, and top-performing property types due to scattered and unstructured data. There is a lack of clear insights into booking patterns, seasonal trends, and customer preferences. This makes it difficult for hosts and stakeholders to maximize revenue and make data-driven decisions.

Without clear insights into customer preferences, payment behavior, and city-wise performance, the company struggles to make data-driven decisions to optimize sales, improve marketing strategies, and maximize overall profitability.

Goal of the dashboard
To provide a centralized and interactive view of Motorola mobile sales data, enabling stakeholders to quickly monitor key metrics like revenue, sales volume, and transactions.

It helps uncover trends, identify top-performing regions and products, and understand customer behavior, ultimately supporting data-driven decisions to improve sales performance and business growth.

Walk through of key visuals
KPI Cards:
Display overall performance metrics such as total profit, average price, number of listings, and hosts, providing a quick snapshot of Airbnb business performance.

Price Trend (Line Chart):
Shows how average price changes over time (monthly), helping identify seasonal trends, peak periods, and pricing fluctuations.

City-wise Pricing (Bar Chart):
Highlights average price across different cities, allowing easy comparison to identify high-value and low-cost locations.

Geographical Analysis (Map Visualization):
Displays location-wise distribution of listings/bookings, helping identify high-demand areas and popular regions.

Property Type Analysis (Bar Chart):
Shows pricing or count by property types (Villa, Apartment, Condo, etc.), helping understand which property types perform best.

Room Type Distribution (Pie/Donut Chart):
Breaks down listings by room type (Entire home, Private room, Shared room) to understand customer preferences.

Neighborhood Analysis (Bar Chart):
Highlights top neighborhoods based on bookings or listings, helping identify high-demand local areas.

Filters/Slicers:
Allow users to interact with the dashboard by selecting city, date, property type, or room type for deeper and customized analysis.

Business impact & Insights
Improved Decision-Making:
The dashboard enables stakeholders to make faster, data-driven decisions by providing a clear view of pricing trends, bookings, and overall performance.

Identification of High-Demand Locations:
Helps identify top-performing cities and neighborhoods, allowing better focus on high-demand areas to maximize bookings and revenue.

Pricing Strategy Optimization:
Analysis of average prices and seasonal trends helps in setting competitive pricing to attract more customers while maximizing profit.

Customer Preference Insights:
Room type and property type analysis reveal customer preferences, helping hosts optimize listings and improve customer satisfaction.

Seasonality & Demand Planning:
Time-based trends highlight peak and low-demand periods, enabling better planning for pricing, availability, and promotions.

Revenue Growth Opportunities:
By identifying underperforming locations or property types, stakeholders can take corrective actions to improve occupancy and overall profitability.
